Initiator sends some string to broker in content field of ACL-Message.
Broker accepts request from initiator, as well as sends it to responder.
After the moment when he got respond, he sends message in content field to responder, where it gets processed.
Spaces are changed to underscores and all letters brought to upper case.
After message were transmitted through broker and processed on responder it will be sent back through broker in processed form.
